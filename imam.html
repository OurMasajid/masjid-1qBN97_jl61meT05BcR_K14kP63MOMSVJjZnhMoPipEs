Sh. Anamul Haq

Imam at Islamic Center of Round Rock

Sh. Anamul Haq is our imam. His formal training includes:

  • Hadith (Jamia Binoria, Pakistan).
  • Arabic Language and Literature (Madrasatul Madinah)
  • Quran memorization (Bangladesh Qaumi Madrasah)
  • Science of Tajweed/Qira'a (Islamabad Nurani Hafizia Madrasah, Bangladesh)

He is fluent in:

  • English
  • Arabic
  • Urdu
  • Bengali

Previous experience includes:

  • Islamic studies coordinator - Calgary Islamic School (Calgary, Canada)
  • Imam positions in various institutions including:
    • Muslim community of Calgary (Calgary, Canada)
    • Muslim Community of Palm Beach County (Florida, USA)
  • Counseling
    • Marriage and family

Beyond the daily salat, he leads and conducts various programs for the education, and spiritual enrichment of the community. With a goal of connecting community members and families living in our contemporary environment with Allah (SWT).
