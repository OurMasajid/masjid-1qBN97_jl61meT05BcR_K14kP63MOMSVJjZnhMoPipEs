Free Covid-19 Testing

Islamic Center of Round Rock is providing free drive-thru Covid-19 testing.

Some Important Information:
Testing is provided by EIXSYS Heathcare System. Results are usually available within 24-48 hours, however, due to high volume, turnaround times may vary.
Testing will be conducted through RT-PCR Diagnostic Panel and specimen will be collected via Nasopharyngeal Swab.

What to bring?
- Insurance Card (for insured)
- Government Issued ID & Social Security # (for uninsured)
- Passport/Government issued ID required (for travelers)

- Date: January 30, 2021
- Time: 10:00AM – 1:00PM
- Address: 1951 Hampton Lane, Round Rock, TX 78664
